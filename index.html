// INSTALL

One command.
That's it.

Parmana detects your hardware, downloads the right model, and sets everything up automatically.

WINDOWS (POWERSHELL)
irm https://raw.githubusercontent.com/EleshVaishnav/PARMANA/main/install.ps1 | iex
click to copy
MAC / LINUX (TERMINAL)
curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/EleshVaishnav/PARMANA/main/install.sh | sh
click to copy
// WHAT HAPPENS
1. Detects your RAM automatically
2. Asks which drive to install on (C, D, E...)
3. Installs Ollama if not present
4. Downloads the best Qwen model for your machine
5. Sets up Parmana personality
6. Optionally sets up Telegram bot
✓ Total time: ~5 minutes
// ABOUT

Why Parmana?

Most AI tools require subscriptions, API keys, or send your data to remote servers. We built Parmana because everyone deserves access to powerful AI — regardless of budget.

Parmana runs entirely on your machine. Your conversations are private. Your data stays yours. No rate limits, no monthly fees, no surveillance.

Built in India, for the world. Supports Hindi, Hinglish, English, and 50+ languages out of the box.
